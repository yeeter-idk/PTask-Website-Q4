Panagbenga Festival

— Baguio City, CAR (Cordillera Administrative Region)

When: February

In Honor Of: The season of blooming flowers in Baguio City

Highlights:
  • Grand float parade decorated with fresh flowers
  • Street dancing inspired by Cordilleran traditions
  • Flower exhibits and trade fairs
  • Cultural shows and performances
Panagbenga Festival (Baguio Flower Festival)
The Panagbenga Festival, also known as the Baguio Flower Festival, is celebrated every February in Baguio City. The word “Panagbenga” comes from the Kankanaey language, meaning “season of blooming.” The festival reflects the rich indigenous culture and creativity of the Cordillera region. Significance: The festival highlights the beauty of flowers and the cultural traditions of the Cordilleran people while attracting many visitors to Baguio City.

Purposes:
  • To celebrate the season of flowers
  • To promote tourism in Baguio City
  • To showcase Cordilleran culture and creativity
