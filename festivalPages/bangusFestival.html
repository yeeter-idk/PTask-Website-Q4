Bangus Festival

— Dagupan City, Pangasinan, Region I - Ilocos Region

When: April (two weeks, culminating end of month)

In Honor Of: Dagupan’s title as “Bangus Capital of the World”

Highlights:
  • kalutan ed Dalan (street party with thousands of grilled milkfish)
  • Gilon-gilon street dancing competition.
  • bangus Rodeo (sorting, weighing competitions)
Bangusine culinary events
The festival celebrates Dagupan’s famous milkfish industry, promotes local aquaculture and tourism, and showcases culinary innovation through vibrant bangus-themed events. Significance: The festival boosts local tourism and the aquaculture industry, attracting hundreds of thousands of visitors to the region annually. 

Purposes:
  • To celebrate Dagupan’s title as the “Bangus Capital of the World”
  • To promote the local aquaculture industry and boost tourism
  • To showcase culinary innovation and cultural pride through milkfish-themed events
