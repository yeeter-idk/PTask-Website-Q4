Pahiyas Festival

— Lucban, CALABARZON

When: May 15

In Honor Of: San Isidro Labrador, the patron saint of farmers

Highlights:
  • Houses decorated with colorful kiping (rice wafers), fruits, and vegetables
  • Street celebrations and cultural performances
  • Display of creative harvest decorations by local residents
  • Food fairs and community festivities
Pahiyas Festival
The Pahiyas Festival is a colorful harvest celebration held in Lucban, Quezon, located in the CALABARZON region. It is known for houses decorated with bright agricultural products and traditional decorations. Significance: The festival expresses the gratitude of farmers for a successful harvest. Homes compete to create the most beautiful decorations using agricultural products, making the entire town vibrant and festive.

Purposes:
  • To honor San Isidro Labrador with gratitude and devotion
  • To give thanks for a bountiful harvest and blessings
  • To promote the culture, creativity, and tourism of Lucban, Quezon
