Pintados-Kasadyaan Festival

— Tacloban City, Region VIII - Eastern Visayas

When: June (around June 29)

In Honor Of: Señor Santo Niño

Highlights:
  • Street dancing with body-painted performers inspired by ancient “pintados” warriors
  • Cultural presentations from different provinces
  • Grand parade and drum performances
Pintados-Kasadyaan Festival
The Pintados-Kasadyaan Festival is a colorful cultural and religious festival celebrated in Tacloban City, located in the Eastern Visayas region. The festival combines two celebrations: Pintados, which showcases the tattooed warriors of Leyte’s history, and Kasadyaan, a regional cultural festival. Significance: The festival highlights the cultural heritage of Eastern Visayas and celebrates the traditions and history of the region through performances and parades.

Purposes:
  • To honor Señor Santo Niño through joyful celebration
  • To preserve and showcase the ancient warrior traditions of Leyte’s “pintados”
  • To promote regional unity through shared cultural heritage
