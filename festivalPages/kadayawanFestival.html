Kadayawan Festival

— Davao City, Region XI - Davao Region

When: Third week of August

In Honor Of: Nature’s blessings and the cultural heritage of Davao’s people

Highlights:
  • Indak-Indak sa Kadalanan (street dancing)
  • Floral float parade
  • Cultural shows featuring the 11 ethnolinguistic tribes
  • Agri-trade fairs and exhibits
Kadayawan thanksgiving celebration
The festival celebrates Davao’s abundant harvest, rich cultural diversity, and the unity of its people. It gives thanks for nature’s blessings while showcasing colorful traditions, agricultural products, and the heritage of the region’s ethnolinguistic communities. Significance: The festival strengthens cultural identity in Davao and boosts tourism, attracting thousands of visitors who experience the region’s traditions, harvest, and vibrant celebrations.

Purposes:
  • To give thanks for nature’s blessings and abundant harvest
  • To celebrate the diverse cultures of Davao’s ethnolinguistic tribes
  • To promote unity, tourism, and cultural pride in the region
