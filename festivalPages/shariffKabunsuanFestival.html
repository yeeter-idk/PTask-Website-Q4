Shariff Kabunsuan Festival

— Cotabato City, BARMM

When: December (usually December 15–19)

In Honor Of: Shariff Kabunsuan

Highlights:
  • Fluvial parade along the Rio Grande de Mindanao
  • Cultural dance and music performances
  • Traditional boat displays
  • Trade fairs and community events
Shariff Kabunsuan Festival
The Shariff Kabunsuan Festival is a vibrant cultural celebration held in Cotabato City, part of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (BARMM). It commemorates the arrival of Shariff Kabunsuan, who introduced Islam to Mindanao, and celebrates the region’s Islamic heritage. Significance: The festival highlights the rich history, faith, and cultural identity of the Bangsamoro people through music, dance, and community festivities.

Purposes:
  • To honor Shariff Kabunsuan and his role in introducing Islam to Mindanao
  • To celebrate the Islamic heritage and cultural identity of the Bangsamoro people
  • To strengthen community unity and pride through cultural performances and festivities
