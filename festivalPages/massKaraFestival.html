MassKara Festival

— Bacolod City, NIR - Negros Island Region

When: October (highlight on the third or fourth weekend)

Theme: “Festival of Smiles”

Highlights:
  • Street dancing with smiling, colorful masks
  • Arena dance competitions
  • Music festivals and concerts
  • Night street parties
MassKara Festival
The MassKara Festival is a vibrant and colorful celebration held every October in Bacolod City, located on Negros Island. It began in 1980 during a time of crisis in Negros to uplift the spirits of the people. Today, it is one of the most famous and lively festivals in the Philippines. Significance: The festival represents the resilience and joyful spirit of the people of Bacolod, expressed through colorful smiling masks, music, and street celebrations.

Purposes:
  • To uplift spirits and promote resilience during challenging times
  • To celebrate Bacolod as the “City of Smiles”
  • To showcase creativity, joy, and community strength through vibrant masks and dancing
