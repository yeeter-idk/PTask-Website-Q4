Bambanti Festival

— Isabela Province, Region II - Cagayan Valley

When: January (2026: January 18–25)

Purpose: Celebrates agricultural abundance and honors farmers

Highlights:
  • Giant Bambanti (creative scarecrow displays)
  • Street dancing competitions
  • Agricultural trade fairs
Cultural performances The word “Bambanti” comes from the Ilocano term for scarecrow, symbolizing protection for crops. The festival brings together towns across Isabela to showcase their culture, honor farmers’ contributions, and celebrate the province’s rich agricultural heritage.
