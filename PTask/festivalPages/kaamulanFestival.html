Kaamulan Festival

— Malaybalay City, Bukidnon, Region X - Northern Mindanao

When: Usually from February to March

In Honor Of: The culture and traditions of Bukidnon’s indigenous tribes

Highlights:
  • Tribal dance competitions
  • Ethnic music and colorful traditional costumes
  • Indigenous rituals and thanksgiving ceremonies
  • Street dancing and cultural presentations
Kaamulan Festival
“Kaamulan” means a gathering, and the festival brings together the seven indigenous tribes of Bukidnon to celebrate their heritage, traditions, and unity through vibrant cultural activities.

Purposes:
  • To celebrate and preserve the culture of Bukidnon’s indigenous tribes
  • To promote unity among the different tribes of the province
  • To showcase indigenous traditions, rituals, and artistry
