Dinagyang Festival

— Iloilo City, Region VI - Western Visayas

When: Fourth Sunday of January

In Honor Of: Señor Santo Niño

Highlights:
  • Ati-Ati street dancing competition
  • Colorful tribal costumes
  • Drumbeats and choreographed performances
  • Religious procession
Dinagyang Festival The Dinagyang Festival is a vibrant religious and cultural festival held every January in Iloilo City, located in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines. It is known for its high-energy performances and well-coordinated street dance competitions. Significance: The festival showcases the rich cultural heritage and artistic excellence of Iloilo while attracting visitors who come to witness its dynamic performances and celebrations.

Purposes:
  • To honor Señor Santo Niño through vibrant devotion
  • To showcase Iloilo’s rich cultural heritage and artistic excellence
  • To promote tourism and community pride through world-class street dancing
