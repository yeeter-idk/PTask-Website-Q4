Zamboanga Hermosa Festival (Fiesta Pilar)

— Zamboanga City, Region IX - Zamboanga Peninsula

When: Every October (highlight on October 12)

In Honor Of: Nuestra Señora La Virgen del Pilar

Highlights:
  • Grand procession and religious activities
  • Street dancing and cultural performances
  • Regatta de Zamboanga (vinta sailboat race)
  • Colorful parades and nightly events
Zamboanga Hermosa Festival
The festival celebrates Zamboanga City’s rich Spanish-influenced heritage and the deep devotion of its people to Our Lady of the Pillar, making it one of the most important religious and cultural celebrations in the region.

Purposes:
  • To honor Nuestra Señora La Virgen del Pilar with deep devotion
  • To celebrate Zamboanga’s rich Spanish-influenced heritage
  • To promote cultural pride and religious unity in the region
