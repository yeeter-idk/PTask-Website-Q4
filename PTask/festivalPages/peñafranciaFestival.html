Peñafrancia Festival

— Naga City, Region V - Bicol Region

When: September

In Honor Of: Our Lady of Peñafrancia, the patroness of Bicol

Highlights:
  • Fluvial procession (river parade) along the Naga River
  • Religious masses and novena prayers
  • Street celebrations and cultural events
  • Millions of devotees attending the festival each year
Peñafrancia Festival
The Peñafrancia Festival is one of the largest religious festivals in the Philippines. It is celebrated every September in Naga City, Bicol Region, in honor of Our Lady of Peñafrancia, the patroness of Bicol. The festival reflects the rich culture and strong religious traditions of the Bicolanos. Significance: The celebration shows the deep faith and devotion of the Bicolano people and gathers millions of devotees who participate in religious activities and festivities.

Purposes:
  • To honor Our Lady of Peñafrancia, the patroness of Bicol
  • To express deep faith and religious devotion
  • To strengthen spiritual unity among millions of Bicolano devotees
