Sinulog Festival

— Cebu City, Region VII - Central Visayas

When: Third Sunday of January

In Honor Of: Señor Santo Niño

Highlights:
  • Sinulog street dancing (two steps forward, one step back)
  • Grand parade and cultural performances
  • Fluvial procession
  • Religious activities at the Basilica
Sinulog Festival
The Sinulog Festival is a grand religious and cultural celebration held every January in Cebu City, located in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It is one of the biggest and most colorful festivals in the country, attracting millions of devotees and tourists every year. Significance: The festival reflects the strong faith, history, and cultural identity of the Cebuano people while bringing together devotees and visitors in celebration.

Purposes:
  • To honor Señor Santo Niño with deep religious devotion
  • To celebrate Cebuano faith, history, and identity
  • To unite millions of devotees and tourists in prayer and festivity
